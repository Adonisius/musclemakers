Introduction

Going to the gym could be a stressful, confusing and overwhelming experience for beginners. Intimidating workout routines, potentially strict diets, and just a general life change could be more than stressful on a newcomer. Our aim is to provide information on muscle groups, dietary information and just general tips to help make the transition into a healthier lifestyle a smooth and welcome experience.
