Introduction

Going to the gym could be a stressful, confusing and overwhelming experience for beginners. Intimidating workout routines, potentially strict diets, and just a general life change could be more than stressful on a newcomer. Our aim is to provide information on muscle groups, dietary information and just general tips to help make the transition into a healthier lifestyle a smooth and welcome experience. Here is a list of different muscle groups, each including their own descriptions and appropriate workouts for each. This website provides simple, easy-to-do and home-frinedly exercises that push anyone and everyone in the right direction.

Biceps

The biceps or biceps brachii is a large muscle that lies on the front of the upper arm between the shoulder and the elbow. Both heads of the muscle arise on the scapula and join to form a single muscle belly which is attached to the upper forearm.
